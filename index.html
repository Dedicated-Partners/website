Who we are.

Dave Parker, Principal

True value creation is measured in absolute terms, either on the bottom line or in the multiple – or both. It is best demonstrated by sustainable, period-over-period improvement across a balanced scorecard of critical business metrics.” With more than 20 years of experience delivering results in a variety of environments that include start-up, rampant growth, and turnaround, Dave Parker has built a career on true value creation.

He currently lives in the Charlotte, NC area with his beautiful wife, Chris, and his two best birthday presents, Logan and James.
